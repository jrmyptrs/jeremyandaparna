Our Story

Our story began in 2015, just as Aparna was deleting her online dating profile. Jeremy had sent his first message to her, asking if her law practice was anything like Saul Goodman’s. Aparna loves a good Bob Odenkirk reference. She decided to give online dating a final try and agreed to a date in Bushwick, Brooklyn.

They agreed to meet at Heavy Woods on 3/28/15 at 7:00pm. Aparna pushed it to 8:00pm. Jeremy showed up 15 minutes early. Aparna arrived 50 minutes late (she still blames the “G Train” for this incident). Sheepishly entering the establishment at 8:50pm, she thought, man, I better be charming to make up for the G Train.

And she was. They talked and laughed for hours. He walked her to the train, ever the gentleman. By the next afternoon, they had arranged their second date. Three days later, Jeremy made Aparna a vegetarian baked ziti. The rest, as they say, was history.
